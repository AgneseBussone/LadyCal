How much your period hurts you? A lot?
And how many painkillers do you take every time? Countless?
Well, a little help during hard times is greatly appreciated but take too much medicines is not a good idea because they can have some really nasty side effects on the short and long term.

Minimal and clean design that show you all the information you need.

Period history

Need to change some information about past periods? Go to your list and change start day, end day or painkiller number.

What's next?

Here, a small list of possible future features that will be added if the developer has time

  • Reminders: show a notification a few days before the expected period, the first day and the last one
  • Pill reminder: for those who use the pill as birth prevention method, a daily reminder to avoid using an alarm set in the clock app
  • PMS: possibility to insert symptoms and monitor their frequency over time
  • Doctor appointment: take note when you'll have your next visit and remind it
  • Notes: notepad to write down what you want to talk about with your doctor
  • Themes: other color combinations
